What's new in miRNASNP-v3 compared to the previous version?

microRNAs (miRNAs) are endogenous and regulatory non-coding RNAs by targeting mRNAs for cleavage or translational repression. SNPs on pre-miRNAs or target sites will affect miRNA expression or functions. miRNASNP is a comprehensive resource of human miRNA-related SNPs, including their effects on target gain and loss.

As the number of SNPs included in the dbSNP data has increased significantly, and many new miRNAs have been discovered in the past few years, we released miRNASNP-v3. In this new version, several new features are added, including:

  • phenotypes for variations located in pre-miRNA and gene 3'UTR,
  • analysis of target gain/loss caused by mutations in miRNA seed and gene 3'UTR,
  • gene enrichment of miRNA targets gain and loss.

And We update data to the latest version, User can

  • search or browse SNPs in pre-miRNA and their impacts on miRNAs' structure,
  • search or browse targets gain/loss by SNP in miRNA seed region and gene 3'UTR,
  • search or browse experiment validated miRNA:gene interaction,
  • search or browse expression and correlation of miRNAs and genes,
  • search or browse expression and correlation of miRNAs and genes.

miRNA information and gene 3'UTR

Information about host gene of miRNAs was extracted from mirna_context.txt file. For miRNAs without host gene we label them as intergenic. A gene 3'UTR file and human genome sequence(GRCh38) were downloaded by the UCSC Table browser, then we use bedtools to get the sequence information for each transcript based on the genome position. To get a more reliable transcript set, gene symbol and refseq mRNA ID corresponding to the transcript ID was downloaded from Ensembl biomart, transcripts which not have gene symbol or refseq mRNA ID were excluded.Finally, transcripts with a sequence length greater than 6(seed region length), 34,116 transcripts of 18,286 genes, were ultimately used for target analysis.

Expression level and expression correlation of miRNAs and target genes

Gene expression data and miRNA expression data of 33 cancers were downloaded from TCGA. We calculated the pearson correlation coefficient between gene and miRNA in each cancer, as well as in 33 cancers. Overall correlation coefficient was displayed in loss targets information panel,which can be clicked to pop out a histogram of correlation coefficient for each cancer type. For gain targets, it's not available, because there are no expression data for SNP type 3'UTR/miRNA.

Linking SNPs to the results of genome-wide association studies (GWAS)

GWAS tagSNPs and SNPs in LD region of the tagSNP(d_prime>0.98) were downloaded from GWAS catalog website for 26 populations. Then, a integrated LD region for tagSNPs relate to a specific SNP of each population is calculated by SNPs in their LD regions. (setting LD analysis regions Â±500kb around SNP position)

Online prediction

In order to reduce the calculation time, only the longest transcript of each gene was used to take online prediction in Tools module.
