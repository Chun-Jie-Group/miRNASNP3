Welcome to the miRNASNP-v3 database

microRNAs (miRNAs) are endogenous and regulatory non-coding RNAs by targeting mRNAs for cleavage or translational repression. SNPs in pre-miRNAs or target sites will affect miRNA function and be related with diseases or biological processes. miRNASNP aims to provide a comprehensive resource of human miRNA-related SNPs, which includes SNPs in pre-miRNAs and target gain and loss by SNPs in miRNA seed regions or 3'UTR of target mRNAs.

miRNASNP-v3 characterized human 6,363,450 SNPs on 2,126 miRNA and 18,152 genes' 3'UTR which doubled compared with miRNASNP-v2. Besides, 203,471 mutations from ClinVar and COSMIC were identified on the miRNA seed region and 3'UTR. Gene enrichment of targets gain/loss by SNP in miRNA seed region are provided for SNP effect on miRNA.

In miRNASNP-v3, you can browse or search

  • SNPs in pre-miRNAs and their impacts on miRNAs. Double!

  • Targets gain/loss by SNPs in gene 3'UTR. Double!

  • Targets gain/loss by SNPs in miRNA seed. Double!

  • Online tools to predict novel SNP effect on miRNA function. Update!

  • Experiment validated miRNA:gene interaction. Update!

  • Expression and correlation of miRNAs and genes. Update!

  • Gene enrichment of miRNA targets gain and loss. New!

  • Mutations on miRNA and 3'UTR from COSMIC and ClinVar. New!

  • Targets gain/loss by mutations in miRNA seed. New!
