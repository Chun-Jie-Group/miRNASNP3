Welcome to the miRNASNP database
MicroRNAs(miRNAs) are studied as key factors in the regulation of gene expression involved in different disease. miRNASNP is a database providing comprehensive resources of single nucleotide polymorphisms (SNPs) in miRNA and genes that may impact miRNA biogenesis and/or miRNA target binding.
Update in miRNASNP version 3

  • Potential functional SNPs increase to 6440089 in version 3 compared to 365994 in version 2

  • Mutations from Cosmic and Clinvar are involed

  • Disease information from GWAS catalog ,Clinvar,Cosmic are instegramed with SNPs

  • Enrichment Analysis of gain/Loss target gene sets are carried out
