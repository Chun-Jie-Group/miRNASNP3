Welcome to the miRNASNP-v3 database

microRNAs (miRNAs) are endogenous and regulatory non-coding RNAs by targeting mRNAs for cleavage or translational repression. SNPs/variations in pre-miRNAs or target sites will affect miRNA expression or functions. miRNASNP is a comprehensive resource of human miRNA-related SNPs/variations, including their effects on target gain and loss.

In miRNASNP-v3, 46,826 SNPs in human 1,897 pre-miRNAs (2,624 mature miRNAs) and 7,115,796 SNPs in 3'UTRs of 18,152 genes were characterized. Besides, 437,997 disease related variations (DRVs) from GWAS, ClinVar and COSMIC were identified in miRNA and gene 3'UTR. Gene enrichment of targets gain/loss by SNPs/variations in miRNA seed region was provided.

In miRNASNP-v3, you can browse or search

  • Targets gain/loss by SNPs in miRNA seed and gene 3'UTR. Update!

  • Online tools to predict novel SNP effect on miRNA function. Update!

  • DRVs impact miRNA target gain/loss and secondary structure. New!

  • Correlation of drug sensitivity and miRNAs. New!
