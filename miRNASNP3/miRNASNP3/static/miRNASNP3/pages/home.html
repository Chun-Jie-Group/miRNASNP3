Welcome to the miRNASNP-v3 database

microRNAs (miRNAs) are endogenous and regulatory non-coding RNAs by targeting mRNAs for cleavage or translational repression. SNPs/variations in pre-miRNAs or target sites will affect miRNA expression or functions. miRNASNP is a comprehensive resource of human miRNA-related SNPs/variations, including their effects on target gain and loss.

In miRNASNP-v3, 6,363,450 SNPs in human 1,897 pre-miRNAs (2,624 mature miRNAs) and 3'UTRs of 18,152 genes were characterized. Besides, 433,269 PRVs were collected from GWAS catalog, ClinVar and COSMIC. 40,892 variations identified in pre-miRNAs have effect on pre-miRNA second structure. In total, 43,770,647 gained targets and 46,347,984 lost targets were analysed inmiRNASNP-v3. Gene enrichment of targets gain/loss by SNPs/variations in miRNA seed region was provided.
