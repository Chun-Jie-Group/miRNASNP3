SNP in miRNA and gene 3'UTR

A total of 46,826 SNPs in pre-miRNAs and 7,115,796 SNPs in gene 3'UTRs were identified by miRNASNP-v3, including 6229 SNPs in miRNA seed regions. For SNPs in miRNA seed regions and SNPs in gene 3'UTRs, we focused on their effects on miRNA:gene interactions, for SNPs in pre-miRNAs, we investigated their effects on pre-miRNA structure. Users can retrieve specific genes/miRNAs to obtain SNPs in them and explore the impact of the SNP on mRNA:miRNA interaction.
