1.Predict the impact of variant in gene 3'UTR on miRNA:mRNA interaction

This part is provided users to predict the loss and gain of miRNA:mRNA binding sites by the variation in 3'UTR, you can input your wild-type sequence and SNP-type sequence, separately.
