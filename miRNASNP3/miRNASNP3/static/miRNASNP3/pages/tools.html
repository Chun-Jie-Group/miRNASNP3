1.Predict the impact of variant in gene 3'UTR on miRNA:mRNA interaction

This part is provided users to predict the loss and gain of miRNA:mRNA binding sites by the variation in 3'UTR, you can input your wild-type sequence and SNP-type sequence, separately. If only wild-type sequence is provided, it will predict miRNAs which target this sequence. If wild-type sequence and SNP-type sequence are provided, you will get miRNA target loss/gain induced by the variant. (Notice: The length of predicted sequence should be less than 5000bp.)

2.Predict the impact of variant in miRNA on miRNA:mRNA interaction

This tool is to predict the loss and gain of miRNA:mRNA binding sites by the variation in miRNA, you can input your wild-type sequence and SNP-type sequence, separately. If only wild-type sequence is provided, it will predict target genes of the miRNA. If wild-type sequence and SNP-type sequence are provided, you will get gene target loss/gain induced by the variant. (Notice: The length of predicted sequence should be less than 30bp. This tool takes a while for predicting, please be patient.)

3.Predict the impact of variant in pre-miRNA on miRNA structure

This tool is provdied users to explore the impact of variant in pre-miRNA on pre-miRNA secondary structure. A SNP list and wild-type pre-miRNA sequence are required.
Input format: The list of SNPs must be given in separate lines. The SNPs are described as, wild-type nucleotide followed by nucleotide position followed by mutant nucleotide (Notice: The length of predicted sequence should be less than 200b).
